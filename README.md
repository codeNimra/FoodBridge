# 🌉 FoodBridge

**Connecting restaurant surplus to food banks — in real time.**

FoodBridge is a real-time marketplace designed to solve the critical gap in the food supply chain. While 40% of food is wasted annually, millions remain food insecure. FoodBridge allows restaurants to post surplus meals instantly, notifying nearby shelters for immediate pickup.

## 🔴 The Problem
- **Waste:** $408B worth of restaurant food is thrown out annually.
- **Insecurity:** 119M Americans are food insecure in 2024.
- **The Gap:** Lack of real-time connection between donors and recipients.

## 🟢 The Solution
- **Real-Time Alerts:** Restaurants post surplus in seconds.
- **Instant Matching:** Automated logistics matching based on location and capacity.
- **Impact Tracking:** CO2 and meal-rescue metrics for brand reputation.
